📖 Analysis Legend & Terminology

Trade Types

LONG_CALL / BULL_CALL_SPREAD: Buy calls or spreads when IV is favorable and bull signals strong. Better than long stock when IV is elevated.
LONG_STOCK: Buy stock outright. Used when bull signals are moderate or IV is too high for options.
WEAK_LONG: Marginal buy signal (net score +1 to +2). Small position only, with caution.
SKIP / HOLD: No edge, conflicting signals, or net negative signal. Do not initiate new positions.

Key Metrics Explained

Net Score: Bull signals minus bear signals. Higher = stronger conviction. Range: -14 to +14.
RSI: Relative Strength Index. >70 = overbought, <30 = oversold. Late-move warning.
MACD: Bullish crossover = momentum turning up. Bearish divergence = momentum fading.
MM Hedge Bias: Sell-on-rally = market makers must sell as price rises (resistance). Buy-on-dips = MMs support dips (support).
Relative Strength: % outperformance vs SPY, QQQ, sector ETF. Leaders usually outperform in bull markets.
Fundamentals Score: 0-14 scale. Strong compounder ≥8, Solid = 4-7, Average = 0-3, Weak ≤ -1.

Technical Levels

Gamma Wall: Strike where MM hedging is heaviest. Acts as price magnet / resistance.
VWAP: Volume-weighted average price. Support/resistance over recent period.
SMA 20: 20-day simple moving average. Primary support in uptrends.
Bollinger Bands: Upper band = overbought resistance. Lower band = oversold support.
Trailing Stop: Percentage-based stop that adjusts as price rises. Recommended: 8-17% depending on volatility.
