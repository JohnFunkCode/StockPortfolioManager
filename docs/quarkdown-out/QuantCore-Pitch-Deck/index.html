Disciplined Exits, Automated

The exit is designed at entry — then it runs itself.

Profit-taking ladders. Sell targets calibrated to each stock’s own volatility — not arbitrary round numbers. As price climbs each rung, an alert fires with the exact shares to sell.

Bail triggers. Every position carries a pre-stated invalidation level requiring multi-signal confirmation — one bad tick doesn’t shake the position; a broken thesis does.

Always-on monitoring. Daily, automatically: portfolio health report, trend-violation and below-cost alerts, ladder-rung notifications — delivered to Discord. The system finds you.

Result in practice: selling 60% of a winning options position into strength — then exiting on the bail trigger — turned a +34% buy-and-hold outcome into +112%.
